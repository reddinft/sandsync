## Setup (do once before recording)

- Find a **quiet room** — no fan, no aircon, no traffic
- Use your **phone mic** held ~15–20cm from your mouth
- **Recording format:** WAV, 48kHz, stereo (highest quality your app supports)
- Do a **10-second test recording** first — play it back and listen for hum or echo
- Speak naturally. These are not corporate lines. You're just a person using an app.
- Save files exactly as named below
